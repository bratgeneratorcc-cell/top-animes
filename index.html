Top 10 Underrated Anime You Should Watch in 2026

Anime culture grows every year, yet many brilliant series never receive the attention they deserve. This list highlights ten underrated anime titles that are worth watching in 2026, especially for viewers who want fresh stories, deeper themes, and new artistic directions.

1. To Your Eternity

A deeply emotional journey about identity, loss, and evolution. It continues to gain respect among story lovers but still flies under the radar for mainstream audiences.

2. 86: Eighty Six

A war drama that connects machine warfare with humanity. It remains overlooked even though critics praise its storytelling.

3. Ousama Ranking

Heartfelt, artistic, and clever. This anime deserves far more recognition.

4. Sonny Boy

Trippy, philosophical, and imaginative. A perfect watch for viewers looking for depth and creativity.

5. Houseki no Kuni

A brilliant CGI masterpiece with themes of identity, survival, and purpose.

6. Odd Taxi

Smart mystery storytelling blended with surprisingly emotional twists.

7. Vivy: Fluorite Eye’s Song

A sci-fi thriller that questions AI, humanity, and destiny. It deserves a comeback in 2026.

8. Wonder Egg Priority

Complex, psychological, and visually fascinating. Despite controversy, it remains unique and memorable.

9. Mushishi

A slow, poetic masterpiece that still doesn’t get universal recognition.

10. Kaiba

Experimental animation meets emotional storytelling. A niche title that greatly benefits from fresh audience attention.
